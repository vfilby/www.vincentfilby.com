Projects

Thamesville: 1925
http://thamesville1925.vincentfilby.com

Years ago I was given copies of a detailed survey map of my home town. The scale was 50 feet per inch and totalled about 36sqft over three sheets. Local historians have annotated nearly every building detailing who lived there, what the building was for and other humorous bits.

I recently found these maps and am working to preserve them digitally and post them for all to see.
